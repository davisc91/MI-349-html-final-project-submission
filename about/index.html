— About Us —

What is VIM?

Founded in 2010, VIM is an entirely student-run fashion, beauty & lifestyle publication at Michigan State University. We publish a bi-annual print magazine, along with two bi-annual digital issues. VIM is more than just a magazine, we strive to be the leading organization on campus for students interested in fashion and media. With a staff comprised of students interested in advertising, graphic design, photography, finance, marketing, public relations, video production, social media, event planning, professional writing & editing, and more, there is truly a place for everyone at VIM. Our mission is to work collaboratively and efficiently to produce the best college fashion publication in the country. We hope to grow together creatively while providing our members with the legitimate experience and necessary skills to succeed in the highly competitive fashion and publishing industries.

FAQ

When are VIM meetings held?

VIM hosts bi-weekly meetings on Mondays from 8 – 9 p.m. Subscribe to our mailing list or follow us on Instagram for updates & meeting recaps.

How can I join?

There is a place for everyone at VIM. Michigan State University students are encouraged to apply for one of our teams or become a general member. All VIM teams hold open applications in the fall, following our first general meeting. Teams with open positions for the Spring semester will accept applications in January. If you’re interested in applying or becoming a general member, you can find more information here.

Does a general member or team member have to pay dues? How much are dues?

Yes, we require all our general members, team members and directors to pay dues. Dues vary each semester. You can pay per semester or annually. Please email vimmagazine@gmail.com to discuss form of payment and amount.

What does being a general member entail?

You benefit and partake in our bi-weekly meetings, semester events, networking opportunities, print and digital issue launches, available merchandise, being part of a boundary-breaking, college publication, meet a lot of cool students and much more!

What does being a VIM team member entail?

We have over 20 teams within VIM Magazine. You will be part of the step-by-step process of producing the digital and printed publications. A single person may only be on one team. You gain responsibility by reporting to your team director, and will have the opportunity to hold a leading role within the organization. Team responsibilities vary per team and director.

Can I contribute to the VIM Blog?

All VIM general members, team members and directors who have paid dues for the current semester can submit to the VIM Blog director to get a chance to publish a byline. For further questions on how to submit to the VIM Blog please reach out to vimmagazine@gmail.com.

Where can I find VIM on campus?

VIM launches its bi-annual print magazine on Michigan State University’s campus and is distributed in the Fall & Spring semesters at no cost to students & faculty. Find VIM at Well’s Hall, Eli Broad College of Business, Communication Arts & Science, Blue Owl Coffee, and more!
